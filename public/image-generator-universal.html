How to Get Clean Images

  1. 1. Select your product above
  2. 2. Click "Open Mercury Forge" (opens in new tab)
  3. 3. Paste the prompt in Mercury Forge
  4. 4. Click FORGE CHROMIUM to generate
  5. 5. Right-click image → Save Image As...
  6. 6. Save to your images folder
  7. 7. Link to your product
